///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
///                    HOW TO PLAY                          ///
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 ATTACKING PLAYER
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Shoot bullets at enemies to destroy them
Each character has a different special that can be used based on a time or ammo limit.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 DEFENDING PLAYER
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Use shields to block bullets of the same colour as your shield.
Swap shield affinities to block the different incoming bullets.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 MISCELLANELOUS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Gather coins from fallen enemies to earn character-specific power ups.
Earn points based on vertical screen location and destroying enemies.

///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
///                    CHARACTERS                          ///
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 PUNCH KNIGHT
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Attack with two punch projectiles fired straight up.
Special utilizes remaining ammo in a massive flurry of punches.
Shield is massive upon role swap and shrinks over time.
Power-ups grant increased damage.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 DRAGON SITTER
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Attack with the bevy of dragons at your disposal.
Special summons a new dragon.
Death takes away one dragon, down to a limit.
Power-ups increase the minimum number of dragons held at a given time.
Each dragon carries its own shield, though all dragons share shield type.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 TINKER
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Attack with any of three bullet patterns.
Bomb (blue) deals an AoE explosion on contact with an enemy.
Homing (red) goes to the nearest enemy.
Piercing (yellow) goes through enemies to hit those beyond.
Special swaps which bullet pattern you're currently using.
Drop shields using your special move while in Defence.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 MIRROR MAGE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Attack using four lasers connected to mirror nodes at various points on your screen.
Use a limit amount of ammo per second while lasers are in play.
When all ammo is gone, the laser will vanish, until ammo is replenished via defence mode.
Defence mode features two shields on either side of the player.
Special fires a projectile straight up into your blind spot.

///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
///                   LEVELS                                     ///
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 LEVEL 1
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Basic enemies who generally colour match with their projectiles.
Limited number of bullets to give the players a chance to get the hang of the basics.
Slime Mage boss summons slimes and utilizes the rain pattern.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 LEVEL 2
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Intermediate enemies have more health and do more damage and don't always colour match.
The bullet hell aspect starts kicking in more severely, and players will need to dodge frequently if they want to survive with enough lives to make it through Level 3.
Gargoyle boss charges the players while trailing behind a hail of bullets.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                 LEVEL 3
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

By far the hardest level, advanced enemies have maximum levels of health and damage and only occasionally colour match.
Bullet hell mode is in full swing here, and the players will need to be very attentive and careful to survive to the boss.  it isn't expected that any but the most skilled bullet hell players will make it through here on their first try or two.
Elemental Mage boss has three phases (fire with turrets, ice with walls, and lightning with bolts), each of which features the games only unblock-able projectiles.  This boss will require many attempts and very careful cooperative play to defeat.

///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
///                   SCORE                                      ///
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Each player builds their individual score throughout the game; the score presented at the end of the game is the combination of the two scores.
Score is intended as a metric for progress, both before the players are capable of beating the 3rd boss and afterwards.  It is certainly not the focus of the game, but it gives a quantitative measure of success which can be useful.
